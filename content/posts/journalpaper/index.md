## Free at Last, Free at Last!

[Read the Paper here!](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijheatmasstransfer.2024.125761)

[Read my actual thesis here!](http://hdl.handle.net/1807/130488)

After nearly an entire year from first submission, dear reader I am delighted to inform you that my first ever journal paper has been published in the International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer (IJHMT)!

This was the grand culmination of my masters thesis condensed in to a single work, and I'm so grateful to my supervisors [Dr. Sanjeev Chandra](https://www.mie.utoronto.ca/faculty_staff/chandra/) and [Dr. Cristina Amon](https://www.mie.utoronto.ca/faculty_staff/amon/) for everything.
