## The Details

I guess we're getting famous? A little while ago, Conifer was approached by Pumps and Systems magazine to write an article on EC motors. Importantly, this was not going to be a marketing piece, but rather a technical article on the benefits of EC motors. At the same time, it was designed for the general reader.

With support from our marketing team (especially Stella Hur who editted and made the figures), I wrote the article over a few weeks. Super fun! Check it out above!
